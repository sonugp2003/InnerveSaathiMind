- FastAPI app (backend/main.py)
- Safety service (backend/services/safety.py)
- GenAI service (backend/services/genai.py)
- Resource service (backend/services/resources.py)

Config & deployment:
- Environment config via backend/config.py and .env
- Optional cloud setup via render.yaml / vercel.json
